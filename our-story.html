About Us

CLUBJETS is more than a venue—it's a movement. We're not just boats and beaches anymore—we're beats.

Created by Jet de la Isla, the visionary behind Puerto Vallarta's top-rated boat tours and immersive nude beach experiences, CLUBJETS MX is the next evolution in queer space-making. Rooted in inclusivity, creativity, and a deep love for community, our space blends co-working by day with techno-fueled, sex-positive nightlife by night.

We're inspired by the wild freedom of Berlin, the sensual elegance of Lisbon, the intimacy of Tokyo bathhouses, and the vibrant spirit of Vallarta itself. After years of bringing queer joy to the ocean, we're bringing it to land—creating a playground for artists, outcasts, dreamers, and lovers of all kinds.

Our programming includes:

  • Genre-less MainStage performances from international and local queer talent
  • Ballroom nights curated by Puerto Vallarta's own community leaders
  • Sex-positive cruise areas rooted in respect and consent
  • Queer art installations & immersive visual experiences
  • Community-building events, workshops & DJ residencies

Whether you come for the music, the movement, the freedom, or the people—you'll leave with something real.

Come work, dance, cruise, connect. Come as you are. Welcome to CLUBJETS MX.

Mission Statement

At CLUBJETS, our mission is to fuse queer joy, creative freedom, and radical inclusivity into a living, breathing space unlike any other. Born from the success of Jet's Private Boat Tours and our legendary Naked Beach events, we've spent years crafting unforgettable experiences at sea—now we're bringing that magic to shore.

We are a sanctuary and a stage for Vallarta's queer voices: a techno-fueled, sex-positive, body-celebrating space where art, music, and connection collide. Our mission is to uplift the outcasts of the outcasts, offering a choose-your-own-adventure space where co-working, cruising, dancing, healing, and belonging all have room to thrive.

From sunrise espresso to midnight lasers—come as you are. Be who you are. You belong here.

Vision Statement

We envision CLUBJETS as a revolutionary cultural epicenter—where the vibrancy of global queer nightlife meets the intimacy of community-building.

Our vision is to expand what nightlife, art, and queer gathering can be. By breaking the binary between day and night, work and play, body and mind, CLUBJETS MX creates a multi-sensory playground that celebrates all forms of queer identity and creativity.

Backed by the highest-rated team in Vallarta's tourism industry, our guides-turned-bartenders light the space with signature Jet hospitality. Anchored by our trusted team of DJs—like DJ Trevor, resident DJ Jameson Rabbit, and local liaison Gallo—we bring a deep bench of sound and soul to every night.

We don't just want to throw parties—we want to start movements.

We're building more than a club. We're building the future of queer joy.
