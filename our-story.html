About Us

Welcome to Club Jets — a bold new playground in Puerto Vallarta's queer underground.

Tucked inside a repurposed warehouse, this space is the brainchild of Jet de la Isla and Chaka Paradise—two forces of nightlife and sex-positive tourism finally coming together on land. After nearly a decade leading Puerto Vallarta's most daring experiences—from five-star boat tours and naked beach takeovers to youth hostels for queer travelers—we've fused our roots, passions, and grit to build something new.

Club Jets is a dual-world experience.

🎶 The front entrance leads to our MainStage: a rotating lineup of neo-reggaetón, ballroom, techno, indie, and experimental queer sounds. Think underground rave meets queer performance art—where DJs, dancers, and local creatives can finally take up space. Want to collaborate? Slide into our DMs.

🖤 The back entrance is SX Club Jets: an all-male fantasy realm of dark techno, playrooms, and labyrinths designed for connection, freedom, and sensual exploration. Voyeurism welcome. Consent is key. Bring your full self.

One warehouse. Two doors. A thousand ways to belong.

Mission Statement

To create a raw, inclusive, and liberating venue where queer music, art, and bodies are celebrated.

We're building the stage that didn't exist—for local DJs, underground artists, and the men craving a safe, sex-positive world of their own.

Vision Statement

We see Puerto Vallarta as a global destination for queer expression, where underground music meets body freedom.

Our vision is to nurture a self-sustaining hub for LGBTQ+ creatives, partygoers, and explorers—rooted in radical authenticity, accessible pricing, and unapologetic joy.
